Life is hard. Picking a wine doesn't have to be!

Our Mission

Our team is devoted to taking confusion and stress out of the wine selection process by providing tailored recommendations and stripping down difficult to understand jargon to create a more inclusive wine tasting community.

Our Values

Inclusivity: It is our belief that you should not have to be a sommelier to choose and enjoy a glass of wine.

Simplicity: We strive for an easy and intuitive design for seamless wine picking.

Trust: BlendBud is completely free to use and does not partner with any wine companies, so you can rest assured that the recommendations we serve you with are completely unbiased.
